VolumeAccessGroup Class
A volume access group is a useful way of grouping volumes and initiators together for ease of management.

VolumeAccessGroup Limits:

- A volume access group can contain up to sixty-four initiator IQNs. - An initiator can only belong to only one volume access group. - A volume access group can contain up to two thousand volumes. - Each volume access group can belong to a maximum of four other volume access groups.
